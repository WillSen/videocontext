How-to make coconut crumble

Preheat oven to 250Â°f and line a 1/4 sheet tray with parchment. Mix 40 g milk powder, 40 g flour, 12 g cornsarch, 25 g sugar, 25 g unsweetened flaked coconut and 2 g salt in a bowl. Add 55 g melted butter and stir with a spatula until crumbly. Put onto a baking tray and bake 20 minutes or until light golden brown. Let cool. Put crumble into a bowl and toss with the 20 g milk powder and 25 g unsweetened flaked coconut until evenly distributed. Add 90 g good quality white chocolate, melted and cooled and stir until clumps form. Spread crumbs onto sheet tray to cool completely.
